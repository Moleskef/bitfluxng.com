WHAT ARE THE COSTS?

Bitflux has a tried and tested business model that provides the Partners with a genuine opportunity to enjoy an attractive return on investment.

Bitflux will build, operate and managed the network while the partners will focus on sales, marketing and customer services.

The initial investment will cover Sales and Marketing, Customer services and premises-related establishment costs, devices and working capital.
